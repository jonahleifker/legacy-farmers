We don't view crop insurance as a static administrative cost. By building dynamic coverage models, we lock in guaranteed revenue floors that protect your margins and validate your marketing plan.

Establishing a Revenue Floor

We specialize in **Revenue Protection (RP)** policies rather than basic Yield Protection. RP protects your operation against both physical yield loss (drought, hail, wind, disease) and market price collapses.

RP uses a harvest price guarantee, meaning if crop prices rise between planting and harvest, your coverage automatically adjusts upward to match the higher price. This is crucial for farmers who forward-contract crop sales.

Stacking Coverages: Stacking SCO & ECO

To limit your deductible and cover shallow losses without paying high farm-level premiums, we design "stacked" coverage structures:

  • Base Policy (RP): Typically written at 75% to 85% coverage, protecting your individual farm production.
  • Supplemental Coverage Option (SCO): Stacks on top of your base policy, covering the band between your chosen level and 86% using county-level triggers.
  • Enhanced Coverage Option (ECO): Extends your protection even further, covering the band from 86% up to 90% or 95% of expected county revenue.

Why Work with Vrzak Insurance?

RMA crop insurance rates are standardized—every agent writes policies at the exact same price. We differentiate ourselves by providing:

Important Dates

Crop insurance is bound by strict federal closing deadlines. For spring-planted crops (Corn & Soybeans):

  • March 15: Sales Closing / Policy Change Date
  • July 15: Acreage Reporting Deadline
  • Dec 10: Harvest Production Reporting
